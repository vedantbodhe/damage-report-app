Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about Report My Damage!

Q: What is Report My Damage!?

A: Report My Damage! is a web service that allows you to submit images of damaged packages, automatically identify the type of damage, store the report, and (optionally) notify the sender via email.

Q: How long does it take to process a report?

A: Once you submit your photo, our system uses AI (OpenAI Vision) to classify the damage in under 10 seconds. We then store your report immediately. If you request email notifications, those are sent within another 30 seconds.

Q: Can I track my report?

A: Yes! Simply click “Track Your Report” from the menu, enter your Report ID, and you’ll see the current status (damage type, timestamp, etc.). We plan to add more detailed status updates—stay tuned!

Q: Is my data stored securely?

A: Absolutely. All images are uploaded to a securely configured S3 bucket. We never share your images or email addresses. For more details, see our Privacy Policy (link coming soon).

Q: Who can I contact for support?

A: Use the “Contact Us” form from the menu or email us directly at support@reportmydamage.com. We’ll respond within 24 hours.
